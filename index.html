Expression Recognition in Cohn Kanade+ dataset

Trained a multi class support vector machine to recognise six facial expressions happy, surprise, sad, angry, disgust, fear by extracting features around the mouth and eyes regions of face.
Used a pyramid of local binary patterns on the low resolution images of Cohn Kanade+ dataset achieving 70% accuracy(highest) for happy and sad expressions.
